About The Study

We are interested in understanding how students approach learning. This research may help shape the future of educational approaches. Your participation is valuable in advancing our understanding of effective learning techniques.

What You'll Do

  1. During the first session, you'll learn about a topic (no prior knowledge required).
  2. One week later, you'll return to complete a brief survey about your learning experience.

Total time commitment: About 2 hours across both sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions

No specialized knowledge is required. The study is designed for all Middlebury students regardless of academic background or major.
